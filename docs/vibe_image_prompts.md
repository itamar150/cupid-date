# Vibe Image Prompts

Use these prompts to generate one premium, realistic, romantic-clean image per vibe.

General constraints for all images:
- Photorealistic, premium, warm, natural, not stock-photo looking
- No text, no logos, no watermark
- Vertical composition suitable for a mobile carousel card
- Leave subtle negative space near the lower center for the vibe label overlay
- Cinematic but clean lighting
- Real materials and believable environments
- No exaggerated fantasy styling

## calm
Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: mobile app vibe card
Primary request: a quiet romantic date atmosphere in a refined lounge corner
Scene/backdrop: intimate indoor setting with soft linen seating, a small candlelit table, warm neutral tones, elegant decor
Subject: a serene premium space that feels calm and emotionally safe for a couple
Style/medium: photorealistic lifestyle interior photography
Composition/framing: vertical frame, eye-level, balanced composition, open space near the lower center
Lighting/mood: soft warm evening light, subtle glow, peaceful mood
Color palette: cream, blush beige, dusty rose, muted taupe
Materials/textures: linen, matte ceramic, soft wood, brushed metal
Constraints: no visible faces, no text, no clutter
Avoid: harsh contrast, empty commercial showroom look, stock-photo staging
